Everything You Need to Know

FAMCO Approved means every piece of equipment has been verified through official OEM channels, undergone a comprehensive 150-point inspection by trained Famco technicians, and received a standardized condition grade (A, B, or C). This certification provides buyers with complete transparency and confidence in their purchase.
Our certified technicians perform a thorough 150-point inspection covering mechanical, structural, electrical, and cosmetic aspects of every machine. The full inspection report is made available to potential buyers before purchase, ensuring complete transparency and informed decision-making.
We offer Murabaha (cost-plus financing) and Ijarah (lease-to-own) structures approved by recognized Shariah boards. These are fully integrated into your purchase journey, compliant with VAT and ZATCA regulations, and feature transparent profit-rate calculations with no hidden fees.
When you make a purchase, your funds are held securely in escrow by a regulated third party. The seller only receives payment after you confirm delivery and verify the equipment matches its description and inspection report. This protects both buyers and sellers in every transaction.

Vehicle Description

This 2023 Volvo FH 500 XL is a premium long-haul tractor unit in excellent used condition. Powered by Volvo's D13K engine delivering 500 HP (375 kW) with Euro 6 emission compliance, paired with the renowned I-Shift automatic transmission for smooth, fuel-efficient operation.

The XL sleeper cab offers a spacious, comfortable environment with full air suspension, aluminium alloy wheels, parking heater, seat heating, leather steering wheel, cruise control, and a comprehensive safety package. This vehicle has been inspected and approved under the FAMCO certified program.
